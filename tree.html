Steps to plant trees

step 1

Choose a good location

Choose a perfect loaction for the
tree you want to plant.

step 2

Dig a smal hole

Make the hole twice as wide as the seedling and deep enough for the roots

step 3

Place the seedlings gently.

Position the plant gently in the hole without breaking the roots

step 4

Cover with soil

Fill the hole and press the soil lightly around the base.

step 5

Water the plant

Water immediately and continue watering consistently
for the first few weeks.
