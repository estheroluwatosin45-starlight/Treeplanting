How to plant trees

step 1

Choose a good location

Choose a perfect loaction for the
tree you want to plant.

step 2

Dig a small hole

Maake the hole twice as wide as the seedlings and deep enough for the roots.

step 3

Place the seedling

Position it gently in the hole without breaking the roots.

step 4

Cover with soil

Fill the hole and press the soil lightly around the base.

step 5

Water the plants

Water immediately and continue watering consistently for the first few weeks.
